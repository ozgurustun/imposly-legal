Imposly - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

Imposly is a party word game designed to be played offline with friends. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Imposly does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, or any personal information. Player names entered during gameplay are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

All game data (settings, language preferences) is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Imposly uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles data.

Children's Privacy

Imposly does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages (4+). Ads shown are configured to be family-friendly.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, gameplay, or behavior in any way.

Internet Connection

Imposly works fully offline. An internet connection is only used to load advertisements. The game itself requires no internet access.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
